Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city that seamlessly blends history and modernity. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate, reflecting its rich historical and cultural heritage. The city is a political and administrative hub, housing important institutions like the Parliament and Rashtrapati Bhavan. Delhi is famous for its diverse and delicious street food, including chaat, parathas, and butter chicken. The bustling markets of Chandni Chowk and Connaught Place offer a unique shopping experience. As a major educational center, the city hosts prestigious institutions like Delhi University, IIT Delhi, and AIIMS. The efficient metro system connects different parts of the city, easing daily commuting. Delhi experiences extreme weather conditions, with scorching summers and chilly winters. The city is also known for its vibrant festivals, including Diwali, Holi, and Eid, celebrated with great enthusiasm. With its rich history, economic significance, and cultural diversity, Delhi remains a fascinating destination for both residents and tourists.
